How to play

  • Start typing.The letters will appear in the boxes
  • Remove letters with Backspace
  • Hit Enter/Return to submit an answer
  • Letters with green backrounds are in the right spot
  • Letters with yellow backgrounds exist in the word, but are in the wrong spot
  • Letters with gray backrounds do not exist in the word
  • If you need a hint, click the ? icon
